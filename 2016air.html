Plane Fun Facts


1 - The engines provide the trust to make the plane move forward. It also helps the wings to create lift making the plane go up.

2 - The body of the plane (where the passengers sit) is called a fuselage.

3 - A Boeing 747 can carry about 60,000 gallons of jet fuel, which weighs about 400,000 pounds.

4 - The world’s fastest plane in the world is the SR-71 Blackbird, it can go up to 3,530 kph!

5 - The Wright Brothers built the first plane that flew in 1903!

Helicopter Fun Facts



1 - Helicopter’s are capable of taking off and landing vertically, because they hover.

2 - The thrust is delivered by spinning rotors, usually one or two blades.

3 - They have one main rotor and a smaller rotor at the back.

4 - They are used in search and rescue, tourism, medical transport, fighting fire, photography, military and much more!

5 - They are very noisey, vibrate a lot, and are much slower than airplanes.
