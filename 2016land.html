Buses Fun Facts



1 - In the 1920's, there were no windows for buses, instead they used tcloths!

2 - School-bus-yellow is very similar to the colour of mangoes that it has been compared to the colour of mangoes.

3 - In the 1930's truck designs were being modified to make school buses.

4 - The largest bus in the world holds 300 passengers! It is in China and is 24 meters long (82 feet), normal buses are around 19 meters in length (64 feet).

5 - School buses are now all equipped with stop signs that pop open when they stop. It warns drivers to stop while children are boarding of off-loading.

Cars Fun Facts

2 - The first cars used a lever instead of a steering wheel.

3 - Airbags only take 40 milliseconds to inflate during an accident.

4 - The very first car was made that was powered by fuel, was made in 1807.

5 - Approximately 95% of retired cars are recycled. Cars are the most recycled product in the world. About 27 million cars are recycled each year.
